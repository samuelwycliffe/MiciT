We Are a Crew

We 4 are aspiring hackers and honestly this is the first time we work as a Team. We worked together and put in all our hardwork effectively and here we present our unified effort in the form of this APK.

We are the First year students of the Sri Krishna College of Engineering and Technology, Coimbatore. We are doing our Bachelors in Engineering in the field of Computer Science. Each one of us are individually skilled in different sub-domains of the vast computer science domain.By putting together all our knowledge, it made the android software development an easy task for our TEAM. We came to the conclusion of choosing the problem statement in the Domain of Networks and Communication. Under this topic we came out with the question, why cant we communicate, without the need of internet, by using a LAN server with a community of people. So what's next .? We started with our project and here we are with our final APK.
